ChatGPT Prompt Frameworks

Unlock the full potential of ChatGPT

R-T-F

Act as [ROLE]: Facebook Ad Marketer
Create a [TASK]: Design a compelling Facebook ad campaign to promote a new line of fitness apparel for a sports brand.
Show as [FORMAT]: Create a storyboard outlining the sequence of ad creatives, including ad copy, visuals, and targeting strategy.

T-A-G

Define [TASK]: The task is to evaluate the performance of team members.
State the [ACTION]: Act as a Direct manager and assess the strengths and weaknesses of team members.
Clarify the [GOAL]: Goal is to improve team performance so that the average user satisfaction score moves from 6 to 7.5 in the next quarter.

B-A-B

Explain Problem [BEFORE]: We're nowhere to be seen on SEO rankings.
State Outcome [AFTER]: We want to be in top 10 SEO ranking in our niche in 90 days.
Ask for the [BRIDGE]: Develop a detailed plan for mentioning all the measures we should take also include list of top 20 keywords.

C-A-R-E

Give the [CONTEXT]: We are launching a new line of sustainable clothing.
Describe [ACTION]: Can you assist us in creating a targeted advertising campaign that emphasizes our environmental commitment?
Clarify the [RESULT]: Our desired outcome is to drive product awareness and sales.
Give the [EXAMPLE]: A good example of a similar successful initiative is Patagonia's "Don't Buy This Jacket" campaign, which highlighted their commitment to sustainability while enhancing their brand image.

R-I-S-E

Specify the [ROLE]: Imagine you are a content strategist.
Describe [INPUT]: I've gathered detailed information about our target audience, including their interests & common questions related to our industry.
Ask for [STEPS]: Provide a Step by Step content strategy plan identifying key topics based on our audience insights, creating an editorial calendar, and drafting engaging content that aligns with our brand message.
Describe the [EXPECTATION]: Aim is to increase our blog's monthly visitors by 40% and Enhance our brand's position as a thought leader in our industry.
